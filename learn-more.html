Your Calorie Plan, Explained

The Two Numbers That Matter

1. Your BMR (Base Energy)

Think of this as your body's "idle mode." It's the energy you burn just staying alive‚Äîbreathing, thinking, and circulating blood. You burn these calories even when you're sleeping.

2. Your TDEE (Total Energy)

This is your **BMR + all the energy you use for activities** like walking, working, and exercising. This is your "maintenance" number to stay at your current weight.
